EVENTS AT NUTECH

SPRING GALA

The Spring Gala is a vibrant event held by the university featuring diverse stalls, cultural displays, traditional dances, captivating performances, and engaging activities .The students are encouraged to participate to showcase their creativity may it be in terms of art,dancing or making delicious food!

RECRUITMENT DRIVE

Each year a day is taken out of the academic calender to REMIND the freshmen and the seniors that the university is not all about studying!All are encouraged to join different clubs along with various activities and fun!

WORKSHOPS & SEMINARS

Engage in insightful seminars and hands-on workshops at NUTECH, designed to expand your knowledge and skills. Learn from experts, explore new ideas, and stay ahead in your academic and professional journey.
